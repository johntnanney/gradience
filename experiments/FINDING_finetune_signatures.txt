CONNECTING TO GEOMETRIC EARLY STOPPING PAPER
================================================================================

Your paper identified:
- Pre-training: "Compression arc" (κ̃ rises then curls)
- Fine-tuning: "Equilibrium" (κ̃ rises rapidly then plateaus)
- Memorization: "Vertical tower" (κ̃ shoots up)

Our findings ADD:
- Test oscillation as memorization signal
- κ growth RATE differentiates regimes
- Slow κ growth + oscillation = insufficient data/regularization

================================================================================
FINE-TUNING DIAGNOSTIC ALGORITHM
================================================================================

At epoch E (after train acc > 95%):

1. Compute test_stability = std(test_acc[-5:])
2. Compute kappa_growth = κ[E] / κ[0]
3. Compute gap = train_acc - test_acc

CLASSIFY:
- If test_stability < 0.02 AND gap < 0.03 → HEALTHY
- If test_stability > 0.05 OR gap > 0.05 → MEMORIZATION RISK
- If κ dropped > 20% from peak → INSTABILITY

ACTION:
- HEALTHY: Continue or stop (converged)
- MEMORIZATION: Add regularization, more data, or stop
- INSTABILITY: Reduce LR, add warmup
